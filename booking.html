Our Home Eye Check-Up Services

  • 1. Professional Optometrist Visit

    A certified optometrist in a white lab coat visits your home and conducts all examinations with modern equipment.

  • 2. Slit-Lamp Examination

    Detect cataract, pterygium, and conjunctivitis accurately using a slit-lamp for detailed anterior eye assessment.

  • 3. Eye Health Assessment

    Identify eye infections like conjunctivitis and surface disorders precisely using slit-lamp imaging.

  • 4. Auto Refractometer Check

    Automatically measure refractive errors to determine accurate eye glass prescription.

  • 5. Trial Set Examination

    Fine-tune your prescription with trial lenses for perfect vision correction and comfort.

  • 6. Vision Acuity Test

    Detect near and far sight issues using standard vision charts during home visit.

  • 7. Intraocular Pressure (IOP) Test

    Measure eye pressure to detect glaucoma risk and prevent vision loss.

  • 8. Personalized Eye Care Advice

    Receive guidance on eye care, lifestyle, and prescription glasses if required.
